Yeang - Leading Modern Style

Yeang JSC is a prestigious and leading brand in the interior design and construction industry, distinguishing itself from other companies in the market by specializing exclusively in the Modern Style. As a pioneer in shaping modern design trends, Yeang offers innovative solutions in material applications, color schemes, and cohesive structural elements within the modern aesthetic. With three years of experience, Yeang owns a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility and follows standardized management processes, supported by a well-trained team. To meet the growing demand of customers across the country, Yeang aims to expand and provide the best services and conveniences. As one of the most active construction firms, Yeang continuously updates real project images to offer customers the most objective assessments of our work.

Yeang JSC extends a partnership invitation to organizations and individuals interested in collaborating in Residential Interior Design services – specifically in the Modern Style.

WHY CHOOSE Yeang?

In Vietnam's increasingly dynamic and diverse interior market, Yeang takes pride in being a leading force and trendsetter in Modern Style design (as recognized by Vietnam Architecture Magazine). Our designs always strive for harmony between functionality and aesthetics, maintaining a consistent design language from structural elements to color schemes. Every living space we create is a true masterpiece—your sanctuary, the place you long to return to after a long, tiring day.
10 Years – One Style
"10 years pursuing modern style, Yeang has firmly established itself as a leader in this design school. With over 400 apartments designed and fully furnished each year, we are one of the most reputable and leading interior design firms.
Strict Process
Owning a 2,000m² manufacturing facility with a strict quality control system from production to service, you can have complete peace of mind when using our full-package design and construction services.
Emphasizing Feng Shui
Feng Shui is always a top consideration in Yeang's modern interior designs, ensuring living spaces align with the homeowner's age and destiny. This helps minimize negative influences and foster prosperity.
Commitment to Progress
Yeang is the only company today that guarantees on-time apartment construction. If the project is delayed beyond the contracted timeline, we commit to a penalty of 1 million VND per day.
